> Mais informações: <https://www.unix.com/man-page/osx/8/apachectl/>.

- Iniciar o job launchd `org.apache.httpd`:

```bash
apachectl start
```

- Parar o job launchd:

```bash
apachectl stop
```

- Parar, e então iniciar o job launchd:

```bash
apachectl restart
```
