> More information: <https://ss64.com/osx/drutil.html>.

- Eject a disk from the drive:

```bash
drutil eject
```

- Burn a directory as an ISO9660 filesystem onto a DVD. Don't verify and eject when complete:

```bash
drutil burn -noverify -eject -iso9660
```
